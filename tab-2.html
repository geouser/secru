LOCATION TRACKING AND AREA THREAT MONITORING

ASSET TRACKING

Track your location, your family or employees for security purposes. Control the movement of your children or employees in business trips by setting location fence and receives an alarm when they go beyond it. And even check the last location of a missing loved one or person of interest.

STRATEGIC INTELLIGENCE MONITORING

Have someone monitor relevant threats and intelligence reports around your route or area of interest while traveling. Then receive close to real time updates for every development.
